Yes. The 20-minute audit and your three priority fixes cost nothing. I only charge if you decide you want help acting on them, and you will see clear pricing before you commit to anything.

The first call is 20 minutes. If you want a deeper look on-site, we book a longer visit, but you get real, usable findings from that first conversation.

No. Plenty of owners take the three fixes and run with them alone. If they want a hand, I am here. There is no pressure either way.
