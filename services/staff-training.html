Before you book

Fair questions.

Do you train on-site or remotely?

Both. Front-line service training works best on-site in Lagos. Standards, SOPs, and lead coaching can be done remotely where it suits your team.

How long does training take?

It depends on team size and goals, but most venues get real change from a focused session or two plus a follow-up. I keep it practical, not a lecture.

Will it disrupt operations?

No. I work around your shifts and can train in small groups so the venue keeps running while your team learns.

Do you train small teams?

Yes. Even a team of three benefits, and small teams often improve fastest because everyone is in the room.
