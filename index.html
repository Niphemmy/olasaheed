Who I am

From the hotel floor to the business behind it.

Nine years ago I started as a security officer at a Lagos hotel. Six years later I was running the entire property: front desk, housekeeping, kitchen, maintenance, security, and events.

That journey taught me exactly where hotels, lounges, and bars quietly lose money, and how to stop it. Today I help venue owners fix operations, design packages that fill slow periods, train their teams, and win the corporate and government contracts most venues never chase.

I am also a business support specialist and writer. I craft winning proposals and bids (90 percent success rate, including a government mandate worth 50 million naira a year), founded the stock-market education platform ASEG, and authored the Roy-Akan Matrix on workplace productivity.

Questions

Good questions, straight answers.

What exactly does a business support specialist do for my venue?

I look at how your venue gets booked, how it runs day to day, and where money leaks out, then I fix it with you: better operations, packages that fill slow periods, trained staff, and proposals that win new contracts. Think of it as an extra pair of experienced hands focused on growth.

Do you only work with hotels?

No. Hotels, lounges, bars, restaurants, and event centres are the core focus, but I also write proposals and bids, run research and academic support, and deliver training for teams outside hospitality.

How does the free 20-minute audit work?

We talk on WhatsApp or a call. You tell me about your venue, I ask the right questions, and you leave with three specific things to fix that week. No pitch, no pressure, no charge.

What does it cost after that?

The audit is free. From there I give you a short quick-win plan with clear pricing, so you decide with the numbers in front of you. Retainers are available for ongoing support.

Where are you based and do you work remotely?

I am in Agege, Lagos, and work on-site across Lagos. Proposals, research, and training I deliver remotely anywhere.

What are ASEG and the Roy-Akan Matrix?

ASEG is a stock-market education platform I founded. The Roy-Akan Matrix is a productivity framework I authored. Both show the same analytical thinking and writing I bring to your business.
