Who I am

From the hotel floor to the business behind it.

Nine years ago I started as a security officer at a Lagos hotel. Six years later I was running the entire property: front desk, housekeeping, kitchen, maintenance, security, and events.

That journey taught me exactly where hotels, lounges, and bars quietly lose money, and how to stop it. Today I help venue owners fix operations, design packages that fill slow periods, train their teams, and win the corporate and government contracts most venues never chase.

I also write winning business proposals (90 percent success rate, including a government mandate worth 50 million naira a year), founded the stock-market education platform ASEG, and authored the Roy-Akan Matrix on workplace productivity.
